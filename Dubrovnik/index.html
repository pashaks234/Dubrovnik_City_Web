Welcome to Dubrovnik

Explore our great city with beautiful scenery, rich with many history & historical places, and awesome acculturation of culture!

Explore the City

Our city has many places to explore and countless opportuniyy to discover some interesting things & facts. So many things you could do at our city. Start your adventure now.

Enjoy scenery

Beauty of our city, good scenery, view of Adriatic Sea in the horizon and awesome Dalmatian Mountains. These all beautiful scenery is very good to wahs your eyes and relaxing your mind.

Rich in History!

Aside form beautiful city and nature, we also had a long unique history, being on the gate of the Adriatic City, making this city become a strategic position for trade routes.

Acculturation of Culture

Dubrovnik has been ruled by different country across the centuries, and it's strategic location on the trade routes has made thi city filled by different people from different culture and religion.
